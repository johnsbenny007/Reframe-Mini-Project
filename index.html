Reframe: Resource WebApp for making Youtube Videos


Reframe is a cutting-edge web application that brings together all the essential video resources in one place.

With Reframe, users can easily convert text to audio, transcribe speech to text, generate thumbnails, and compress file sizes.

This innovative platform is designed to streamline the video creation process, making it easier and more efficient for users to produce high-quality content. At Reframe, we understand the importance of having access to a wide range of video resources. That's why we've integrated all the essential tools into a single, user-friendly web application. Whether you're a content creator, marketer, or business owner, Reframe has everything you need to create engaging and impactful videos.

Text to Audio

Our text to audio feature allows users to convert written content into high-quality audio files, making it easy to add voiceovers to videos or create podcasts.

Speech to Text

Our speech to text feature transcribes spoken content into text, making it easy to add captions or subtitles to videos.

Thumbnail Generator

Our image generator feature allows users to create custom images for their videos.

File Size Compressor

Our file size compressor feature helps to reduce the size of video files without compromising quality.
